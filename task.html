Simplifying Bus Travel with Technology

The Telangana State Road Transport Corporation (TSRTC) has taken a significant step towards revolutionizing the bus travel experience for commuters. With the introduction of the “TSRTC Gamyam” bus tracking app, passengers can now enjoy real-time information about bus arrivals and departures, making their journeys more efficient and hassle-free.

  • Enhanced Travel Coordination: TSRTC emphasized that the “TSRTC Gamyam” app goes beyond providing bus schedules. It offers precise information about bus arrivals at stops closest to various destinations, be it homes, businesses, shopping centers, events, or transportation hubs like airports and train stations.
  • User-Friendly Platform: The mobile application is available for free download on the Google Play Store, ensuring easy access for Android users. Additionally, it can also be downloaded directly from TSRTC’s official website.

With the launch of the “TSRTC Gamyam” app, the TSRTC is reaffirming its commitment to enhancing the passenger experience by integrating technology into everyday bus travel. This innovative solution not only streamlines travel but also empowers commuters to make well-informed decisions, ultimately making their journeys more efficient and enjoyable.

Types of TSRTC Buses

In total, TSRTC operates buses on around 36,593 routes. However, not all routes are serviced by TSRTC daily. Some of these TSRTC routes are small-length, while others are inter-state routes. One can view the availability of TSRTC coaches on any road with redBus. Complete TSRTC route information is offered by redBus to individuals. TSRTC has many depots and bus stops that help them cover more routes efficiently. In addition, its massive fleet of more than 9,000 coaches can cover many routes.

Travellers who make a TSRTC online booking on redBus tend to choose from the following popular routes commonly:

  • Hyderabad to Karimnagar :The distance between Hyderabad and Karimnagar in Telangana is an average of 170 kilometres (105 miles). It typically takes between 2 and 4.5 hours to cover by road depending on the traffic and road conditions.
  • Bengaluru to Hyderabad :The distance between these two cities is a minimum of 560 kilometres (348 miles), depending on your bus route. It can take between 8 and 14 hours to make the journey by bus.
  • Vijayawada to Hyderabad:The most common route that connects these cities is 275 kilometres (171 miles) long and will take at least 4.5 hours to cover by road.
